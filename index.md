## What is OCRP?

The Open Climate Resilience Policies (OCRP) initiative provides municipalities, organizations, and communities with access to proven climate resilience strategies. Our repository includes model policies, implementation guidance, and real-world examples from jurisdictions that have successfully adapted to climate challenges.

Whether you're addressing urban heat, flood resilience, renewable energy mandates, or other climate adaptation measures, OCRP offers the resources you need to build resilient communities.
