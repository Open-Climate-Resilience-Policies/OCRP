# About OCRP

## Our Mission

Open Climate Resilience Policies (OCRP) provides freely accessible, evidence-based climate adaptation policies to help communities worldwide build resilience in the face of climate change.

## What We Offer

- **Model Policies:** Ready-to-adapt policy templates covering various climate resilience strategies
- **Real-World Examples:** Case studies of successfully implemented climate policies from jurisdictions around the globe
- **Implementation Guidance:** Practical resources for adopting and customizing policies to local contexts
- **Evidence Base:** Links to scientific research, official sources, and news coverage supporting each policy

## How to Use OCRP

Browse our [Policy Library]({{ site.baseurl }}/policies/) to find climate resilience strategies relevant to your community. Each policy includes:

- Policy overview and objectives
- Implementation requirements and timelines
- Expected benefits and co-benefits
- Official sources and supporting documentation
- Related policies and resources

## Contributing

OCRP is an open-source project. We welcome contributions from climate professionals, policy experts, researchers, and community advocates. Visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Open-Climate-Resilience-Policies/OCRP) to contribute.
